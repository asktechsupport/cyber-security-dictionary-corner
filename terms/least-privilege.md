# Least Privilege

## Definition

Least Privilege is the principle of granting users and applications only the permissions required to perform their intended tasks.

## Why It Matters

Limiting permissions reduces the impact of compromised accounts and helps prevent attackers from moving laterally through a network.

## Example

A helpdesk technician can reset user passwords but cannot modify firewall rules or access financial systems.

### Related Terms

- IAM
- RBAC
- Privileged Access
- Zero Trust
