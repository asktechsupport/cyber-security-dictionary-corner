# Alert

## Definition

An alert is a notification generated by a security tool indicating activity that may require investigation. Alerts are created when predefined rules or analytics detect behaviour matching known threats or suspicious activity.

## Why It Matters

Security Operations Centre (SOC) analysts spend much of their day investigating alerts to determine whether they represent genuine threats or normal business activity.

## Example

Microsoft Sentinel generates an alert after detecting multiple failed sign-in attempts followed by a successful login from an unusual location.
