# True Positive

## Definition

A true positive is an alert that correctly identifies genuine malicious activity requiring investigation or remediation.

## Why It Matters

Accurately identifying true positives enables security teams to respond quickly and minimise the impact of cyber attacks.

## Example

An EDR alert detects ransomware encrypting files on an endpoint. Investigation confirms malicious activity, and the device is immediately isolated.
